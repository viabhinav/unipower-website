UNXT1090

The Stabilizers are designed and manufactured under strict and high quality standards which make this an ideal guard of your equipment from Voltage fluctuations. Unipower Digital Voltage Stabilizer is manufactured at India’s Largest ISO 9001/14001 certified Stabilizer plant and is a result of intense research and development undertaken to provide the best to the customers.

Specifications

Input Range  :100V - 280V
Low/High Cut Off     :60V /290V
Working Range  :70V - 280V
Application  :Mainline
Installation Type   :Floor Mounting
Warranty   :2 Years
Warranty Type   :On site

Features

  • Microprocessor Based Design
  • CSS ( Controlled Synchronized Switching ) Technology
  • Smart Thermal Management
  • Time delay Relay
  • High / Low voltage Cutoff
  • Separate MCB for Stabilizer On/Off and By pass
  • Advanced Digital Display
